Biography

Peng Sun is currently a Postdoctoral Researcher with The Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen, advised by Prof. Jianwei Huang. He received his Ph.D degree from Zhejiang University in 2020, advised by Prof. Zhi Wang and Prof. Zhibo Wang, and his B.E. degree from Tianjin University in 2015. From Nov. 2018 to Nov. 2019, he was a visiting Ph.D student with The University of Tennessee, Knoxville, where he conducted research under the supervision of Prof. Hairong Qi.


Research Interests

Peng Sun's research interests lie in incentive mechanism design, task allocation, privacy protection, and data aggregation in networked sensing systems. The research goal is to achieve efficient, reliable, secure, and privacy-preserving data collection in Internet of Things (IoT) and cyber physical systems (CPS). Previously, he conducted research on compressive sensing and its applications in sensor networks and array signal processing. Currently, he is particularly interested in federated learning.
